Help!

Additional resources to help safe guard yourself, or report crimes.

I am not ethically cleared to help any issues that are ongoing, I can however point you to people that can help you.

ActionFraud are a UK based organization that will help you in the event of Fraud & Cyber Crime. By signing up to their system you will be able to create a report, track the progress of the report, input additional information, call them, and receive updates by email. They also provide a lot of resources and information to safeguard users online.

They provide these services in Welsh & English to make sure they can target as many people across the UK as possible.

All users should be constantly vigilant about the possibilites of Cyber Crime for more information on things to be aware of be sure to check ActionFraud's campagin page for regular updates of ongoing campaigns battling CyberCrime.

In the event of a serious crime that is currently happening, always call 999.

Help is available

Users should never stay quiet of the issues they are facing online. By reporting crimes you are helping to make sure these crimes do not happen to another person.

Be sure to report crimes.

In 2018 there was 34.1 million reported Fraud and Computer Misuse reported crimes as reported by the Office for National Statistics.
