Attack Types

Aircrack

A technique used in order to gain access to Wireless Networks via precision techniques, or bruteforcing.

Man in the Middle Attack

Also known as sniffing, a technique used to capture packets between different clients on a network, then be able to retrieve information from these captured packets.

SQL Injection

A versatile tool that gains access to Databases, typically used to gain access to user account information.

Social Engineering

A series of different techniques that allows for attackers to gain more information about a target to be able to retrieve information from them, that can then be used against them to breach them, or a platform that they are within.

Remember

Understanding how these attacks are composed allow for you to gain a better insight as to how you can protect yourself, and others from these types of attacks.
