Why?

In today’s climate Cyber Security is an essential part of all businesses, and an everyday part of our lives. Without this consideration life would be very different allowing for Cyber Criminals to run rampant across the Internet. With the correct protection, and legislation put in place it ensures that the Internet is a safer place for everyone. If you need more help, be sure to check our Help section for more information.

How does that affect me?

Whether you use the Internet for social media, or for your business needs; your privacy and safety are a huge consideration by many organisations. Organisations have the responsibility to offer a safe and secure environment for their users. Sometimes this isn’t enough however – as technology develops Cyber Security threats becoming growingly more sophisticated, and dangerous. Understanding these threats can help to protect not only yourself, but others online.

As a Business how does this affect me?

Data Breaches can be a very expensive ordeal to have deal with a Business. With legislation in place such as GDPR it enforces all businesses to have appropriate protection in place for their client’s information. Failure to enforce this can result in hefty fines, and other fees. Not only can this be financially damaging, it can also impact on the integrity of a Business. Should a business face a Cyber Security threat it can also impact the day to day schedule of a business, for example Ransomware (WannaCry) crippled the NHS in 2017 by encrypting all data that was on NHS Computers, and forcing for them to pay approximately $300 to decrypt files on the Computer.
